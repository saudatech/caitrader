Unit of Conversions

Mass conversions

1 Bale = 170 Kg

1 Qtl (or QTL) = 100 Kg

1 MT = 1000 Kg


Price conversions

₹/Bale = 170 * ₹/Kg

₹/Bale = (1 / 2.09188) * ₹/Candy

₹/Bale = (170 / 37.325) * ₹/Maund

₹/Bale = (170 / 100) * ₹/Qtl

₹/Bale = (170 / 1000) * ₹/MT

₹/Bale = (1 / 1.2807) * ₹/lb

₹/Bale = Forex Rate * USD/Bale

₹/Bale = 170 * Forex Rate * USD/Kg

₹/Bale = (1 / 0.26681935849) * Forex Rate * USC/lb

Invoice Calculator

Inputs: Quantity, Rate, Tax

(1) Gross = Quantity * Rate

(2) IGST = Gross * (Tax / 100)

(3) SGST = IGST / 2

(4) CGST = SGST

(5) Net = Gross + SGST + CGST

Or, Net = Gross + IGST
